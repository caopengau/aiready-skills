# AIReady Marketing Campaign: "The AI Code Standard" (Q1 2026)

**Goal:** Recruit elite developers and forward-thinking founders to establish AIReady as the standard for AI-native code quality.
**Duration:** 8 Weeks (2 months)
**Cadence:** 2 Posts / Week (Tuesday & Thursday)

## Target Audiences

1. **The Architect / Tech Lead:** Cares about long-term health, preventing the "AI Tsunami", and objective gating in CI/CD.
2. **The Vibe Coder / Solo Founder:** Cares about sustained velocity, burning tokens efficiently, and not hitting the "Month 3 Wall".

## Core Narrative Arc

* **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): The Wake Up Call.** Exposing the hidden cost of AI code.
* **Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4): The Metrics.** Why old metrics fail and what "AI-Ready" actually means.
* **Phase 3 (Weeks 5-6): The Solution.** Visualizing the mess and fixing it with tooling.
* **Phase 4 (Weeks 7-8): The Standard.** Call to arms to join the community and build the future of dev standards.
